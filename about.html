Our story

From a single bay to the busiest body shop on Jericho.

Mike Sulek opened Huntington Collision in 2013 with one frame rack, one paint gun and a deal with a friend's tow yard. He'd been working bodies on Long Island for fifteen years already — the deal was simple: take care of the car, take care of the customer, and pay the rent.

Twelve years later we still answer the phone, still write estimates by hand on the first visit, and still hand the keys back with the car detailed. Some things you don't outgrow.

How we work

Four things we don't compromise on.

01

OEM-spec repair

We follow the manufacturer's procedures, not the shortcut. Welds, sealers, primers — all to spec.

02

Honest estimate

The number we write on day one is the number you pay on day done. If something changes mid-job, you hear from us first.

03

Direct with insurance

GEICO, Allstate, Progressive, State Farm, USAA, Liberty, Travelers — we file the photos and write the supplements ourselves.

04

Lifetime warranty

Workmanship on collision repairs is guaranteed for as long as you own the vehicle. We mean it.
